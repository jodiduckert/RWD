ABOUT US

HISTORY

Back in the days before air-conditioning, farmhouses would often have a separate building where they would cook meals, preserve garden-fresh produce, and bake goodies. Having a separate building meant the women would not have to heat the main house while the food was being prepared. This building was known as the summer kitchen.

The summer kitchen was usually a cozy place. It was a great place to gather. It was where family and friends would gravitate when they came to visit, because they knew that warm smiles, good food, and friendly conversation were guaranteed.

Today, we have air-conditioning and modern kitchens with all the conveniences. But, we still need a place to go once in a while where warm smiles, good food, and friendly conversation are guaranteed. So, with that in mind, don't heat up the kitchen in your house tonight. Head out to the Summer Kitchen.

We'll be waiting for you with a big plate of something good to eat.

Guaranteed.

FAQ

SERVING SIZE

Q: How many people will a gallon beverage serve?
A: A gallon will serve 8 12-ounce servings.

Q: How many people will a quiche serve?
A: Our quiche is baked in a 9 inch pie shell and will serve 5 to 6 people.

Q: How many people will a quart of food serve?
A: A quart of food will serve 6 to 8 people as a side dish. . If the quart is for a main dish, it will serve 2-3. A quart of soup will serve 2-3.

Q: How many people will a fruit or vegetable tray serve?
A: We usually suggest that you order enough for 3-5 pieces per person.

Q: What are your casseroles served in?
A: The Summer Kitchen Cafe casseroles are served in two different sized aluminum pans. A 10" x 12" pan will hold up to 10 servings and a 12" x 20" pan will hold up to 25 servings.

Q: What is the minimum number of servings you need to place a catering order from the casserole or salad menu?
A: You need to place an minimum order of 10 servings per casserole or salad.

Q: What is the minimum number of Box Lunches you can order at one time?
A: You must order a minimum of 8 box lunches at one time.

Q: How many people does a 300 piece cheese and cracker tray serve?
A: The Summer Kitchen Cafe suggests that you allow 3 to 4 servings per person, so a 300 piece tray will serve 37 to 50 people.

PRICING

Q: How much are your paper products and what do they include?
A: Paper products are $.50 per person. They include a set of plastic silverware, a napkin, a plate, and a cup if applicable.

Q: How much is your delivery fee?
A: For large catering orders, we will happily deliver your food. We charge $15.00 per delivery. If it's outside the Omaha area, it's a little more. Call us. If you are a school or church and you are ordering over $100 worth of food, we'll waive the delivery fee.

INGREDIENTS

Q: What is a stratta?
A: A stratta is a baked egg dish, with a layer of fresh sliced potatoes lining the bottom, along with a meat, a cheese, and a vegetable of your choice.

Q: Can we substitute potato chips for fruit on the Box Lunches?
A:Yes, you can substitute potato chips for fruit on the Box Lunches with no additional charge.

Q: What are croissant puffs and what are they stuffed with?
A: Croissant puffs are1/3 of the size of our regular croissant and can be stuffed with your choice of homemade chicken salad, tuna salad, ham salad, turkey, roast beef, or ham.

Q: What is an aztec pie?
A: An aztec pie is a layered dip served with homemade tortilla chips. The dip consists of refried beans, green onion, black olives, sour cream, diced tomatoes, and guacamole.

Q: What kind of muffins does the Summer Kitchen Cafe make on a daily basis?
A: We make a blueberry, raspberry, cherry almond, morning glory, bran, and a low-fat muffin each day. We make a specialty muffin on the weekends.

Q: What is the portion size of potato casseroles?
A: One portion size of our potato casserole from the catering menu is a little less than the junior size potato casserole from our main menu.

TIMING

Q: How long will the product stay hot in the aluminum pan?
A: Most of The Summer Kitchen Cafe casseroles will stay hot for approximately 15 minutes in the pan we provide as long as it stays covered.

Q: How much advance notice does the Summer Kitchen Cafe need to prepare our catering order?
A: If possible, we ask that you give The Summer Kitchen Cafe a one day advance notice to prepare your catering order. Quiche Bites, Aztec Pie, and Cheesecakes are some of the few items that require a 48 hour advance notice.
