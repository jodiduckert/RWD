SCOOTER'S INFO

Scooter's Coffeehouse and Summer Kitchen...a delicious partnership.
A few years back, Summer Kitchen and Scooter's Coffeehouse decided to get together. We made an amazing discovery: Scooter's premium coffee and smoothies, paired with Summer Kitchen's delectable treats made for an exciting dining experience. At any of our locations you can order from Scooter's extensive menu while eating in Summer Kitchen's Dining Room. Don't have time to stop? Speed through our drive-thru window and treat yourself.

What could be better than that?
